
ScreenShots:
- Screenshot of game is taken
- Screenshot is generalized
- Values are extracted, saved for Input
- screenshot is given to AI


Reinforcement:
- Positive reinforcement for higher health
- Positive reinforcement for higher power cubes
- Positive reinforcement for less remaining brawlers (or teams)
- Negative reinforcement when health drops
- potentially Negative reinforcement over time if less teams and less cubes

Inputs:
- Health 
- Power cubes
- Teams(players) remaining
- Image of Screenshot

Outputs:
- wasd
- e (to shoot)
- f (to super)
- q (to gadget) {future development}
- shift (to emote) {future development} [End of match only, for fun lol]
